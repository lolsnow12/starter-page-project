Who We Are

Bridging the
Literacy Gap

The Page Project was founded on a simple truth: access to books shouldn't depend on a child's zip code. We organize community book drives, partner with schools and libraries, and ensure that donated books reach the children who need them most.

From picture books to young adult novels, every donation is sorted, tracked, and delivered with care — because every story has the power to change a life.
